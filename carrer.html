Internship at 1mg.com

1mg is India's best online pharmacy with a wide range of Prescription and OTC medicines. It provides you with medical information which is curated, written and verified by experts, accurate and trustworthy. Our experts create high-quality content about medicines, diseases, lab investigations, Over-The-Counter (OTC) health products, Ayurvedic herbs/ingredients, and alternative remedies.

  • Location: PAN India
  • Stipend: 10% of the delivered order value for 1mg
  • Start Date: 16th Mar 2020
  • Duration: 4-8 Weeks
  • Industry: Pharmaceuticals, Ecommerce, Healthcare
  • Function: Lead Generation, Sales, Marketing
  • Rewards: Certificate of excellence with Logo

Selected intern's day-to-day responsibilities include:
  1. Look for new users to place the orders via unique coupon codes.
  2. Get fellow college-mates to use 1mg platform to buy their medicines.
  3. Support will be provided with respect to product knowledge, promotional content such as posters, etc.
Selected interns will be provided with unique coupons codes that they will be using to get orders placed.

New users will get 20% discount with this code and existing customers shall get 20 + 5 % discount.
